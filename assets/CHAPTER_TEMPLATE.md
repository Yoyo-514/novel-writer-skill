# Chapter Template - Chinese Scene-First Drafting

## Chapter Targets

- Language: Simplified Chinese
- Chapter length: about 3000 Chinese characters
- Scene count: 2-4 scenes, default 3
- Scene length: about 700-1500 Chinese characters
- POV: third-person limited unless configured otherwise

## Scene Structure

Each scene should have:

- concrete opening image
- POV anchor
- active goal
- pressure, obstacle, or question
- information change
- emotional movement
- exit hook or transition

## Chapter Assembly

1. Draft scenes separately.
2. Check each scene against the outline and character bible.
3. Join scenes in causal order.
4. Smooth transitions without erasing meaningful scene breaks.
5. End with a turn that makes the next chapter necessary.

## Prose Rules

- Write natural Chinese prose, not translated English.
- Avoid process language such as "this scene shows".
- Avoid padding for word count.
- Let exposition appear through conflict, action, dialogue, or perception.
- Keep character voices distinct.
